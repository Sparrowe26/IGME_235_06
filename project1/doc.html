Notes

Goal: Tell a narrative story through the form of a fictional travel article.

The audience could be anyone interested in fantasy worlds, so when it comes to design/organization, I want the audience to be able to formulate the story of the world being described with no prior knowledge of the source.

Challenges:
This design was particularly challenging due to the extra floating text boxes all throughout the page. In order to achieve this, I created a grid overlapping the entire page by using absolute positioning. It took me a while to figure out, but once I did, positioning of those elements became much easier.

Mobile Layout:
Constructing the mobile layout I planned was more difficult than I thought, so I ended up simplifying it slightly as I went. Especially because the text boxes seemed like they would be much too small with the tentative layout.

Navigation:
Navigation was a bit difficult as links didn't work if they were positioned underneath the floating grid. So, I had to also add it to the grid.
