Portal

Publishing Info

  • by: Buka Entertainment
  • Developed by: Valve Corporation
  • Released: Oct 10, 2007

Description

Portal is a first-person puzzle-platformer where you play as Chell, a test subject forced to complete a series of tests utilizing the Aperture Science Handheld Portal Device, or "portal gun."
Set in the Half-Life universe, Chell is trapped in the Aperture Science facility with a malicious AI named GlaDOS and many puzzles to solve to aid in her escape.

"Portal will always be known as one of the best written games of all times."
-Patrick Bregger
